## 💬 MODULE 6: OPERATIONAL & CUSTOMER SUPPORT FAQs

#### Q: How do I access my tracking dashboard?
**A:** You can access the standard tracking dashboard by visiting [https://tracking.translinket.com](https://tracking.translinket.com) or download our **Translink Pro** Android app from the Google Play Store using your customer credentials.

#### Q: How does the fuel monitoring system detect fuel theft?
**A:** Our system uses capacitive Fuel Level Sensors (FLS) to constantly monitor fuel volume in the tank. If a sudden drop in fuel level is detected while the vehicle is parked or moving, the system triggers an immediate **Siphoning Alarm** via email, portal alerts, and mobile push notifications.

#### Q: What is an FTA-certified speed limiter?
**A:** It is a mandated speed limiting governor integrated with a GPS tracker. It keeps vehicle speeds within legal boundaries and feeds real-time telemetry straight to the Federal Transport Authority's national databases. Translink is fully authorized to supply and install these devices in Ethiopia.

#### Q: What happens if the driver is drowsy or distracted?
**A:** The DMS (Driver Monitoring System) camera detects fatigue indicators (such as micro-sleeps or consecutive yawns) and triggers a loud local audio alarm inside the vehicle cabin. Concurrently, a video clip of the incident is securely uploaded to the fleet manager's dashboard as a critical safety event.
